Anti-Vocale transcribes voice messages from WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, and other messaging apps directly on your Android device. All processing happens on-device — your audio never leaves your phone.

No network connection, no cloud, no accounts, no tracking. AI models run locally via ONNX Runtime.

CHOOSE YOUR MODEL

Multiple swappable ASR backends, each with different tradeoffs:
- Parakeet TDT: Fast multilingual, 25 European languages
- Whisper Distil Italian: Best Italian quality, optimized for voice messages
- Whisper Turbo: 99 languages, balanced speed/quality
- Whisper Small: Compact, 99 languages
- Qwen3-ASR: Compact, 52 languages
- Nemotron 3.5 streaming: Progressive text display in real time
- Gemma 4 E2B/E4B: Multimodal AI, 140+ languages

FEATURES

- Share voice messages from any app via the Android share sheet
- Auto-copy transcription to clipboard (paste straight back into chat)
- Auto-save transcripts to a folder of your choice (Drive, Syncthing, Dropbox)
- Video file transcription (extracts the audio track)
- Progressive display — text appears in real time as it decodes
- Per-app notification preferences (auto-copy, quick share back)
- Transcription log with search and swipe actions
- Silence stripping and parallel chunk processing for speed
- Tasker integration for automation

PRIVACY

No network, no accounts, no data collection, no ads. The audio never leaves your phone. Models download once from HuggingFace; pick one and you're set.
